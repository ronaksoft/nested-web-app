Summary of Service

Communications happen in PLACEs and users in each place are either: Creator, Insider or Member. Creators have the highest privileges in the place itself and all the child places. Users of child places are a subset of the parent place users. Places could be accessed by non-members. Although it is controlled by creators of the place. Depending on the place settings non-members can either have read, write or both access. Creators authority overrides in child places

Messages composed by users are owned by the targeted places and senders have no control over their posted messages unless they get their authority from the place which owns the post.
